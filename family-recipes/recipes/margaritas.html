Margaritas

by Jennifer Segal

The perfect margarita: shaken with fresh lime juice, a good tequila, and orange liqueur. Salt the rim. Don't skip the fresh lime.

Serves 1
Total 5 min
photo coming soon

Ingredients

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz orange liqueur (Cointreau, Triple Sec, or Grand Marnier)
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice (from 1 lime)
  • ¼ oz simple syrup or agave syrup (optional)
  • Lime slices, for garnish
  • Coarse salt, for rimming the glass

Instructions

  1. Place coarse salt on a small plate. Rub the rim of a glass with a lime wedge and dip in the salt. Fill the glass with ice.
  2. Combine the tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice, and syrup in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake vigorously, then strain into the prepared glass. Garnish with a lime slice.
