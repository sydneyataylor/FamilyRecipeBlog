Christmas Strada

The reason Christmas morning always smells incredible. Assemble it the night before and it takes care of itself while presents are being opened. Easy enough to be a tradition.

Serves 8
Prep 20 min + overnight
Cook 55 min
Total ~9.5 hrs
photo coming soon

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 lb pork sausage
  • 6 slices bread, cubed
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground dry mustard

Instructions

  1. Lightly grease a 7×11-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the sausage until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain.
  3. Layer the bread cubes, cooked sausage, and shredded cheese in the prepared baking dish.
  4. Beat together the milk, eggs, salt, and dry mustard in a bowl. Pour evenly over the bread mixture. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
  5. The next morning, preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Let the casserole sit at room temperature for at least 30 minutes before baking.
  6. Bake until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 50 to 60 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

Ham or bacon work just as well as sausage. For a different version, add cooked chopped broccoli to the layers.
