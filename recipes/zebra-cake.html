Zebra Cake

An icebox cake — chocolate wafer cookies layered with vanilla whipped cream and left in the fridge overnight. The magic is in the diagonal slice. A classic for a reason.

Serves 6
Prep 20 min
Chill 8 hrs+
Total 8 hrs 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 (9 oz) package chocolate wafer cookies
  • ¼ cup grated chocolate

Instructions

  1. Beat the cream in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer. Gradually add the confectioners' sugar and vanilla, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  2. Spread a generous teaspoon of whipped cream onto each cookie.
  3. Press the cookies together to form 3-inch stacks.
  4. Spread whipped cream in a 1-inch-wide line down the center of a serving platter. Turn the cookie stacks sideways and join them into a log; place in the whipped cream on the platter to secure.
  5. Frost the cookie log with the remaining whipped cream and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Cover tightly and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  6. To serve, slice diagonally to reveal the striped pattern inside.
